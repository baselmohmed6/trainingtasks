Personal trainers have unreal knowledge.

Max gym personal trainers are selected because they are at the top of their game

Personal trainers help you achieve your goals quicker.

People who train with a personal trainer work, on average, 20% harder than if they did a similar training session alone.

Brent Carter, SSC
Head Coach

Brent Carter has been a Starting Strength Coach since 2013 and a Seminar Staff Coach since 2014, coaching Starting Strength Seminars across the US. Prior to his involvement with Starting Strength he received his degree in Exercise and Sports Sciences from Texas Tech University with emphasis in biomechanics and exercise physiology.Brent has been coaching clients of all populations and with various goals since 2004.

Jordan Burnett, SSC
Strength Coach

Jordan Burnett has been in the Fitness industry since 2017. He developed a roster of clients from varying demographics, using barbell training to get them stronger and move them toward their fitness goals. Jordan was taken on as an apprentice coach under Brent Carter, SSC at Starting Strength Dallas when they opened in July of 2019, and went on to earn the Starting Strength Coach credential in March of 2020.
